About forwarding

Forwarding sends a copy of incoming mail to an external address. The source must be a local address on a registered domain.

Tip: enable Keep Copy to retain a copy in the local mailbox while also forwarding.

The local email address to forward. The domain must be registered in the Domains section. Any valid email address where mail should be forwarded, including external addresses. When enabled, mail is delivered to both the local mailbox and the forwarding destination.
